Dr. Krishnaveni Achary

{#
Founder, Creative head
#}

She is a certified Autism trainer with more than a decade of experience dealing with mild, moderate, and severe levels of children. She is a PhD Holder from Delhi university with a specialization in Autism. She has vast experience dealing with children, teenagers, and adults with Autism and NDD. She associates and works for bringing the attitudinal difference on the core difficulties like the theory of mind deficits through play therapy. Training through technology innovation and vocational skills are also her areas of expertise.


Mr. Niranjan Kumar

{#
Founder, Executive Director
#}

He is a 22 years experienced speech therapist with specialization in speech language assessment and therapeutic intervention for children with Autism, down syndrome, global development delay, apraxia, cerebral palsy, fluency disorder, articulation disorder. He also empowers parents with teaching tools and training for the children with Special needs along with one to one therapies for children at his centre Darpan : An image of Innocence.


Mrs. Mona Goyal

{#
Founder, Director, Treasurer

She is the face of LeeshaNoblesouls whose experience as a mother leads to a greater ability to develop vision and wisdom. She is empathetic and highly energetic with greater communication abilities.
